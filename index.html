VISHWAS FOUNDATION

FOR THE PHYSICALLY CHALLENGED

About Us

Vishwas Foundation for the Physically Challenged is a non-governmental organization that was founded in 2020, with the aim of empowering the physically challenged individuals in India. Our organization strives to improve the physical, mental, economic, social and spiritual well-being of the physically challenged population. According to the census conducted in 2011, 2.21% of the Indian population is physically challenged and they face numerous challenges in society. The physically challenged individuals in India struggle to achieve economic independence due to limited job opportunities and difficulties in finding employment. We believe that providing skill development and vocational training can aid in bridging this gap and help in achieving their economic independence. Additionally, we provide support to enhance their physical, social and mental well-being by organizing various Para sports training, events and workshops.

Vission

To create an inclusive society where physically challenged individuals are empowered and have equal opportunities to achieve their potential.

Mission

To empower Physically Challenged and underprivileged people through developmental initiatives focusing on Para sports, Educational, Social, Economic, Cultural and Technological aspects.

Values

Our values are Drive, Passion, Integrity, Teamwork & working with Transparency.

Our Services

Empowerment

To empower physically challenged individuals by providing them with skill development and vocational training, which can aid in achieving their economic independence.

Inclusion

To promote social inclusion of physically challenged individuals by organizing events and workshops that foster social interaction and improve mental wellbeing.

Advocacy

To advocate for the rights and welfare of physically challenged individuals by partnering with government agencies, NGOs, and other stakeholders to create awareness about their challenges and promote policy changes that benefit them.

Accessibility

To promote accessibility by identifying and addressing barriers that physically challenged individuals face in public spaces, transportation, and other areas.

Collaboration

To collaborate with other organizations, groups, and individuals who share our vision to create a network of support for physically challenged individuals and their families.

Programs

The Vishwas Foundation for the Physically Challenged runs five flagship programs aimed at empowering and improving the lives of physically challenged individuals in India. These programs are:

Swavalambi Program (Self Employment)

This program provides vocational training in trades such as tailoring, diya painting, cement pot-making, garland making, handicrafts and product packing. The main objective is to encourage self-employment and entrepreneurship among physically challenged individuals.

Sporti Program (Sports Program)

The Sporti Program aims to promote Para sports in India and aims to win medals at the 2024 Paralympics. Physically challenged individuals are provided with training in wheelchair basketball, table tennis, and swimming.

Pratibhe Program (Art Program)

The Pratibhe Program aims to bring out the hidden talents in physically challenged individuals, such as singing, dancing, flute-playing, one-act plays and drama. The program provides opportunities for these individuals to showcase their talents and promotes such great talents.

Prerana Program (Education Program)

The Prerana Program promotes primary and higher education along with distant education making physically challenged individuals fit for various competitive exams. The program also supports blind students by providing scribes for exams and audio recordings of textbooks.

Aashreya Program (Residential Program)

The Aashreya Program aims to start wheelchair-accessible hostels for physically challenged individuals, such as education aspirants, sportspeople, working men, and women. The Vishwas Foundation for the Physically Challenged is aiming to uplift the physically challenged community by providing them with a safe and accessible place to live.
